🔓 Unlock & Rewards System

Our app offers premium font styles that can be unlocked:

  • Free Styles: Always available without any unlock
  • Locked Styles: Unlock by watching rewarded ads (24-hour access)
  • Renewals: You can renew unlocked styles up to 7 times per day

Unlock information is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. No server storage.

💾 Data Storage

Local Storage

  • Theme Preferences: Your chosen theme (dark/light/system)
  • Unlock Status: Which styles you've unlocked and expiry time
  • Ad Counters: How many times you've used free features

No External Storage

We do NOT upload any data to external servers. All your text, generated content, and preferences stay on your device.
